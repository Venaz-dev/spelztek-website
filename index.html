Augmented Reality

Augmented reality overlays digital content and information onto the physical world as if they're actually there with you, in your own space.
AR opens up new ways for your devices to be helpful throughout your day by letting you experience digital content in the same way you experience the world.
It lets you search things visually, simply by pointing your camera at them.
It can put answers right where your questions are by overlaying visual, immersive content on top of your real world.
